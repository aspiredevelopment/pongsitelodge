Tsuglagkhang Complex

Tsuglagkhang Complex "–" Located on the Temple Road, the Tsuglagkhang Complex is one of the most celebrated religious centers and a popular point of interest in Dharamshala. It was built in the year 1959 when His Holiness the Dalai Lama visited our country for the first time. The complex is one of the most popular places to visit in Dharamshala & encompasses of Dalai Lama"’"s abode and a Tibetan monastery. Numerous cultural events & performances are held in this complex. Public & private sessions of Dalai Lama are also hosted.
