The Hotel

Pong Site Lodge

Located near the Dal Lake at the entrance of famous Strawberry Hills, Hotel Shiva Sanctuary is the Best Hotel to Stay in Mcleodganj that provides its guests with comprehensive hospitality services in the lap of nature.
Hotel Shiva Sanctuary is located at a prime location surrounded by numerous tourist destinations. You can conveniently visit places near our establishment & create eternal holiday memories.
Feel the divinity & serenity of mother nature as our rooms provide you with beautiful views of the snow-capped peaks of the Dhauladhar Range. One of the most famous Mcleodganj Market is located just 4 Km away from the hotel while the famous Naddi sunset viewpoint is just 800 meters away. Our hotel is counted amongst the Best Honeymoon Hotels in Mcleodganj that provides you with extraordinary hospitality & catering services.
Our hotel is suitable for small or large groups and families. Dining options, recreational facilities, and an exclusive range of amenities are also available. We give exemplary importance to health & hygiene.
